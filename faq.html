FAQ:

Q: My computer has been hacked! Can Eric's PC Help?

A: Yes! However, we may need to request certain personal information, we will only do this in person, not over the phone.

Q: How much does a typical gaming computer cost?

A: It can vary quite a bit, however you can expect between $600 and $1200 for a mid to high range gaming PC.

Q: Can Eric's PC help over the phone?

A: Yes! We offer remote support and use Google Meet so you can share your screen with us.

Q: My Internet is slow, can Eric's PC help?

A: We can provide best practices and check your connection speed, however if you rent a device, we do ask that you contact your ISP for any device related questions.
